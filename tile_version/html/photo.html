Ever since I returned from my study abroad program in the wonderful city of Istanbul, I fell in love with photography. What started out as simple entertainment from getting that perfect postcard shot quickly transformed into something so much more. I learned that by capturing the right shot I can capture the viewer, bringing them into a different world in which they can immerse themselves in, even for just a moment.

I'm constantly experimenting with different methods; I believe that it's the best way to grow and find my own style. My shots have been featured by companies like Google and Ariva, travel blogs, art galleries and various other photo competitions and I am always open to collaborate and start something new. To see more of my work, feel free to explore my photography page.
