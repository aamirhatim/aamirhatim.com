As a research assistant, I worked with graduate and PhD students studying stem cell behavior on man-made cell scaffolds. My main responsibility was to create these scaffolds using a resin based 3D printer and CAD software (Rhino). I also built a new printing tray to print moe consistent scaffolds for testing.
